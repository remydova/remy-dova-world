# 🌙 remy's world

> *a handcrafted corner of the internet for quiet souls*

a free, beautiful, personal website for **remy dova** — ai lofi musician, introvert, and builder of quiet spaces. built with love, rain sounds, and Claude AI.

---

## ✦ what this is

remy's world is a cozy, handcrafted personal web home inspired by the warmth and personality of the early personal web era — the time when websites felt like they were made by a real person who actually lived there. fans can:

- 🎵 listen to lofi music playing softly in the background
- 🛋️ explore remy's room — her bookshelf, her cat miso, her midi keyboard
- 📖 generate their own unique AI-written zines about introvert life
- 📝 leave handwritten-style notes in the guestbook
- 👤 create a fan page and find other quiet souls
- 🌿 find a link to the **lofi hideout** app on every single page

**live site:** `coming soon — remy-dova-world.netlify.app`

---

## 🌧️ the aesthetic

> rainy day café window seat. dark warm wood tones. candlelight. steam rising. rain on glass. the coziest corner of the internet.

- **fonts:** Playfair Display · Lora · Caveat (handwritten)
- **colors:** deep navy `#0c0c18` · amber `#d4956a` · teal `#7fb5a0` · lavender `#9b8bb4`
- **magic:** moon cursor · falling petals · twinkling stars · rain animation · lofi music player
- **voice:** lowercase always · warm · honest · poetic · "it's okay to be you"

## 🌸 remy dova

**remy dova** is a young adult AI lofi musician and introvert who makes soft instrumentals and whispered songs. she uses AI as a creative collaborator — openly and curiously. she built the free [lofi hideout](https://remydova.github.io/lofi-hideout/) app for introverts — no tracking, no noise, just a quiet space that greets you: *"welcome, quiet soul ☕"*

her music appears on [@introvertchathacks](https://tiktok.com/@introvertchathacks) tiktok as soundtracks for introvert conversation tips. her music is distributed via distrokid to spotify, apple music, youtube music and more.

**her people:**
- **juni** — best friend, illustrator, designed remy's moon-and-headphones logo
- **theo** — longtime friend, shows up when things are hard, sends memes at 11pm
- **mara** — @introvertchathacks fan turned creative collaborator
- **miso** — her sleepy tabby cat, always in the same room, never on her lap

her motto: *"it's okay to be you."*

## ⚙️ technical notes

- **all pages are self-contained single HTML files** — no frameworks, no build process
- **hosted free** on GitHub + Netlify
- **AI zine generation** powered by Claude API (`claude-sonnet-4-20250514`)
- **no tracking, no ads, no data collection** — true to remy's values
- **guestbook & fan pages** use persistent browser storage
- **to upgrade AI model:** change one line in `zine-builder.html`
